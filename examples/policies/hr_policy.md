# Human Resources Policy

## Time Off and Leave

### Vacation Time
- Employees accrue 15 days of vacation per year
- Vacation requests must be submitted at least 2 weeks in advance
- Maximum 5 consecutive days without manager approval
- Unused vacation days do not roll over to the next year

### Sick Leave
- 10 paid sick days per year
- Doctor's note required for absences over 3 consecutive days
- Sick leave can be used for employee or immediate family member illness

### Remote Work
- Hybrid schedule: 3 days in office, 2 days remote per week
- Full remote work requires VP approval and annual review
- Remote employees must maintain dedicated workspace and reliable internet

## Performance Reviews
- Annual performance reviews conducted in Q4
- Quarterly check-ins with managers
- Performance improvement plans for employees below expectations

## Code of Conduct
- Professional behavior expected at all times
- Zero tolerance for harassment or discrimination
- Conflicts of interest must be disclosed immediately
